Become an Editor

The Editor will work with the reviewers & other editors to oversee the editorial process for submissions. The role involves a wide array of editorial responsibilities, from daily submission assessments to the development of editorial policies.

The position offers an opportunity for candidates looking for a move from research into scholarly publishing, as well as those seeking to advance their editorial experience. Applicants should be versatile, able to adapt to fast-paced environments and show a proven interest in editorial matters and the scholarly literature.

We are looking to broaden the skills covered by our editorial team and would particularly welcome candidates with experience in the related journals.

Responsibilities

  • Assessing new submissions and guiding manuscripts through the review process
  • Overseeing editorial decisions
  • Development of editorial policies and processes

Qualifications and Experience

  • Experience in a scientific research environment
  • Highly organized
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills, both written and oral
  • A PhD qualification is desirable but not essential

If you would like to edit a journal, please send us a full proposal regarding the journal including:

  • Journal Name
  • Your complete academic CV
