Journals

Avestia publishes a number of academic journals, all of which are peer reviewed and maintain high standards while being based on the Open Access model. Each journal has an ISSN, all articles have individual DOIs, and every issue will be submitted to Google Scholars, DRJI, Microsoft Academic Search, Academic Index, Mendeley, and Bielefeld Academic Search for indexing.
